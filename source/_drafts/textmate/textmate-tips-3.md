In this post, we will learn about various kinds of shortcuts in TextMate 2. Shortcut is the prime force of productivity!

##Common Shortcuts

<br/><br/><br/>
###File

|shortcuts|description|
|---------|-----------|
|⌘n| create new window|
|⌥⌘n|create new tab|
|⇧⌘n|***create new file***|
|⌘o|show open files window|
|⇧⌘o|***open favorites***|
|⌘w|***close current tab***|
|⇧⌘w|close window|
|⌃⌥⌘w|***close all tabs***|
|⌃⌘w|***close other tabs***|
|⌘s|save|
|⇧⌘s|save as|
|⌥⌘s|save all|
|⌘p|print|
